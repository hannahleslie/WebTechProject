Ontario Gardening

2017

This year was hard year for gardening in Ontario, Canada. We had a lot of rain and cloudy days. There were a lot of tropical plants this year that suffered, but many thrived from the moister in the soil.

Hosta

The hosta was least affected by the rain. It loves the shade from all the overcast days. It loves the damp setting and really thrives!

Read more

Purple Bells

These love the sun! They also love rain. It needs both the rain and sun to sustain. Too much rain and it will turn yellow and look sad. Keep an eye on these ones.

Read more

Blue Bells

The blue bells love the rain. They do not need much sun so this year was good for them. If we have a similar summer again then you may want to add these to your garden.
